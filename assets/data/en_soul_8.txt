Those with soul number 8 in their name place primary focus on material assets.

This can be anything from money to legal matters, to power and wealth. You may experience more hunger for attaining success than other people. Your soul urge is to gain mastery over the physical world.

However, in the pursuit of attaining material wealth, you may lose sight of the bigger picture.

Though your drive and ambition can help you succeed, it can also push you to take extreme action. Even though you are good at making money, you may also be prone to losing it just as quickly. You must find a balance between your ambition and the bigger picture to attain happiness.
